---
title: Download for iOS & iPadOS
description: Sideload the Manabrew mobile app on iPhone and iPad with SideStore.
---

Manabrew is not on the App Store. The iOS / iPadOS app is distributed as a
sideloaded build, so you install it through **[SideStore](https://sidestore.io)**,
which re-signs the app with your own Apple ID — no paid Apple Developer account
required.

## 1. Install SideStore

:::note
You **must** install SideStore first — it is what lets you sideload the app onto
iOS or iPadOS. There is no way to install Manabrew on iPhone/iPad without it (or
another AltStore-compatible sideloader).
:::

Follow the official setup guide at [sidestore.io](https://sidestore.io). It walks
you through pairing your device and installing SideStore itself. You only do this
once per device.

## 2. Add the Manabrew source

In SideStore, open the **Sources** tab, tap **+**, and add:

```
https://play.manabrew.app/sidestore/apps.json
```

## 3. Install Manabrew

Go to the **Browse** tab, select **Manabrew**, and tap **Install**. SideStore
signs and installs it to your home screen.

:::note
On a **free** Apple ID, sideloaded apps expire after 7 days — open SideStore and
tap **Refresh** (My Apps) before they lapse. A paid Apple Developer account
extends this to a year.
:::
